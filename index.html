Our Story

First time we saw each other was in the university. We studied computer science together and pretty much hangs out as friends. Fast forward, 2 years, we decided to be in a relationship and ever since, we're going strong.

Willy

We both have big dreams, Steffi's dream is to study abroad, which came true in 2014. We had to go through the 6-hour time-difference LDR. With the huge support from Steffi, the next year, Willy got the chance to study abroad too! We were so happy because, even though we're not in the same city, at least it became easier to talk, as we only had 1-hour time-difference. Fast forward to now, we're finally living in the same city.

What was your most memorable date?

We spent one of our first dates going to the TMII, spending the time playing around with different exhibitions. You might think that we were both geeks. And maybe you're right, because we spent the time playing like a kid and had lots of fun :)

What's the most memorable trip you have taken together?

Two years ago, we went to Cinque Terre, Italy. That was the first time we did such a long hiking together. We spent hours to reach the destination. We were super tired but we had a great teamwork there :)

What's the best meal you have eaten together?

Ramen! Willy is a huge fan of ramen, and we really like the Japanese authentic Ramen in Berlin, called Takumi :)
